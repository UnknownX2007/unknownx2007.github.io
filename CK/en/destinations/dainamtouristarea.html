Tourism - Entertainment Complex

Although located in Binh Duong (bordering HCMC), Dai Nam is a complex tourist area with many records. It combines spiritual tourism (Golden Palace), entertainment (water park, games), and a zoo.

  • Address: 1765 National Highway 13, Hiep An Ward, Thu Dau Mot City, Binh Duong.
  • Opening Hours: 08:00 - 17:00 (weekdays), 08:00 - 18:00 (weekends)

Main Areas

Dai Nam has an extremely large area, including many sections:

  • Golden Palace: A large, majestic temple with many details said to be plated in 24K gold, the spiritual center of the tourist area.
  • Artificial Beach: The largest artificial beach in Vietnam, with a wave system like a real beach.
  • Open Zoo: Home to many rare animals, including white tigers, rhinos...
  • Amusement Area: A variety of thrill rides like roller coasters, spinning rides, and an indoor game area.

Visitor Tips

Note: Dai Nam is very large; you should use the electric car or train service within the area to get around. There are many types of tickets (gate ticket, combo tickets, individual ride tickets), consider your needs to buy the appropriate one.
