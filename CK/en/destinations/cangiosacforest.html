Saigon's Green Lungs

Recognized by UNESCO as a world biosphere reserve, Can Gio is an ideal destination to escape the city's noise. It's famous for its mangrove ecosystem, Monkey Island, and the Rung Sac revolutionary base.

  • Address: Can Gio District, HCMC (approx. 50km from center)
  • Opening Hours: 07:00 - 17:00 (daily)

Unique Experiences

Coming to Can Gio, you will immerse yourself in wild nature:

  • Visit Monkey Island: Home to thousands of long-tailed macaques, very bold with humans.
  • Canoe through the forest: Explore the canals, visit the revolutionary base and the crocodile farm.
  • Forest trekking: Enjoy the fresh air of the mangrove forest.
  • Can Gio Beach: Enjoy fresh seafood at the 30/4 beach.

Visitor Tips

Note: When visiting Monkey Island, guard your belongings (phones, cameras, hats) carefully as the monkeys are very mischievous and may snatch things. It's best to go for a day trip and return before dark.
