Gothic Architectural Masterpiece

The Notre-Dame Cathedral, or Basilica of Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception, is one of the most unique and famous architectural structures in HCMC, attracting tourists with its ancient beauty.

  • Address: 01 Cong xa Paris, Ben Nghe Ward, District 1.
  • Visiting Hours: 8:30 - 10:30, 14:30 - 15:30 (Mon - Fri)

Architectural Highlights

The structure was built in a modified Romanesque and Gothic style, creating a harmonious and solemn beauty. All building materials were imported from France.

  • Marseille Bricks: All exterior wall tiles were imported from Marseille (France), and still retain their original bright pink color today.
  • Bell Towers: The two bell towers, nearly 58m high, are the main feature. Initially, the cathedral did not have bell towers; they were added in 1895.
  • Stained Glass: The window system is decorated with vibrant stained glass panels, depicting characters and events from the Bible.

Admission & Visiting Hours:

  • Admission: Free
  • Tourist Visiting Hours: 08:00 - 11:00 & 14:00 - 16:00 (Daily)
  • Sunday Mass (English): 09:30 AM
Note: This is a religious site. Visitors should wear polite, modest clothing (covering shoulders and knees) and maintain silence when visiting inside the cathedral.
