National Historical Relic

The Cu Chi Tunnels are a complex underground defense system, symbolizing the resilient and indomitable will of the Vietnamese people. This is a unique military marvel with a system of tunnels, trenches, and fortifications... stretching hundreds of kilometers.

  • Address: Phu Hiep Hamlet, Phu My Hung Commune, Cu Chi District.
  • Opening Hours: 07:00 - 17:00 (All days)

Experiences at the Tunnels

When visiting the Cu Chi Tunnels, visitors not only explore the tunnel system but also participate in many activities recreating wartime life:

  • Simulated Base Area: Watch documentaries and dioramas simulating battles.
  • Tunnel Crawling Experience: Challenge yourself by crawling through a tunnel section recreated (and widened) for tourists.
  • Shooting Range: Experience shooting at the national defense sports shooting range (supervised).
  • Field Cuisine: Enjoy boiled cassava with sesame salt, a typical dish of the old guerillas.

Visitor Tips

Note: The tunnel is quite narrow, dark, and stuffy. You should reconsider if you have claustrophobia. Wear comfortable, active-friendly clothing and sports shoes.
