3 "Authentic" Saigon Eating Tips You Should Know

October 25, 2025 | Posted by: Admin

Saigon is a culinary paradise, but to fully enjoy the flavors, you need to know a few "secrets". Here are 3 small tips to help you have the best street food experience.

1. Eat where the locals crowd

Don't be afraid of sidewalk stalls. If you see a place crowded with local people, that's a sign of a delicious and affordable eatery.

2. Try Vietnamese iced coffee

Coffee is not just a drink; it's a culture. Try a traditional filtered iced coffee at a roadside stall to feel the slow rhythm of the city.

3. Don't miss the alleys

Many culinary "gems" are hidden in small alleys. From noodle soups, and offal stew, to various sweet soups, Saigon's alleys hold the most original flavors.
