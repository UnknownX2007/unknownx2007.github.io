A World of Endless Entertainment

Dam Sen is one of the largest and oldest amusement parks in HCMC. It includes two main areas: Dam Sen Dry Park (with games, zoo) and Dam Sen Water Park (with pools, slides).

  • Address: 262 Lac Long Quan, Ward 5, District 11.
  • Opening Hours: 08:00 - 18:00 (weekdays), 08:00 - 21:00 (weekends/holidays)

Must-Try Activities

Dam Sen serves all ages with a variety of activities:

  • Water Park: A complex of pools, a lazy river, and thrilling water slides.
  • Dry Park: Includes a zoo, aquarium, traditional games area, and thrill rides like roller coasters, giant Ferris wheel.
  • Stage and Events: Regularly hosts music performances, circuses, and major festival events.
  • Dam Sen Lake: Experience paddle boating or taking a boat trip on the central lake.

Entrance fee:

  • Adults (over 1.4m): 220,000 VND
  • Children (0.8m - 1.4m): 180,000 VND
  • Children (under 0.8m): Free
  • Opening Hours: 08:30 - 18:00 (Mon-Sat), 08:00 - 18:00 (Sun)
Note: Dam Sen Dry Park and Water Park have separate tickets. You should buy a combo ticket if you want to experience both. The park is very large, wear sneakers and bring swimwear if you go to the water park.
