A Perspective on History

As one of the most visited museums by international tourists in Vietnam, this place displays documents, photos, and artifacts about the consequences of aggressive wars, especially the Vietnam War.

  • Address: 28 Vo Van Tan, Ward 6, District 3.
  • Opening Hours: 07:30 - 17:30 (daily)

Main Exhibition Areas

The museum is divided into many thematic areas, both indoors and outdoors:

  • Outdoors: Display of large military artifacts like the F-5A aircraft, M48 tank, 175mm artillery.
  • Indoors (3 floors): Themes on "Historical Truths," "Agent Orange Consequences," "War Crimes," and "Stories of Victims."
  • "Tiger Cages" Area: Recreates a part of the Con Dao prison system.
  • "The World Supports Vietnam" Display: Exhibits on anti-war movements worldwide.

Entrance fee:

  • Adults: 40,000 VND
  • Children (6-15 years): 20,000 VND
  • Children (under 6 years): Free
  • Opening Hours: 07:30 - 17:30 (Daily)
Note: Some images at the museum can be very disturbing. Consider this when visiting with children. You should spend at least 1.5 - 2 hours to see the entire museum.
