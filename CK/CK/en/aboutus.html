About Us

Project "Ho Chi Minh City - Places to Go"

Welcome to the most vibrant heart of the country, where modern life blends with historical imprints and rich cultural identity. Here, you can start your day with a fragrant iced milk coffee in a small alley, stroll through ancient French colonial architecture, and end the evening amidst the bustling atmosphere of Nguyen Hue Walking Street or Ben Thanh Night Market.

From unique street food, colorful traditional markets, to contemporary art spaces and a vibrant nightlife, Ho Chi Minh City captivates visitors from the very first moment.

Let's explore every corner, every flavor, every story – to see that this place is not just a destination, but an unforgettable experience.
